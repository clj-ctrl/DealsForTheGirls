Deal Literacy
by CJ Jondonero

The deals your clients talk about,
finally explained.

You work in hospitality. You hear words like "capital stack" and "waterfall" in meetings, on calls, in the decks you're helping build. And you nod — because that's the job.

But here's the thing: when you actually understand what those words mean, everything changes. You ask better questions. You write sharper copy. You earn a seat at a different kind of table.

This isn't a finance course. It's a fluency guide — ten short lessons that explain how real estate deals actually work, written the way a colleague would explain it over coffee. No formulas to memorize. No MBA required.

CJ
