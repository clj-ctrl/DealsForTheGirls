Deal Literacy
by CJ Jondonero

Nobody explained
the money part.
Let me.

You're in a meeting. Someone says "the capital stack is fully committed" or "we're targeting a 16% IRR" and the room nods like that means something. You nod too. You move on.

This is for you.

If you work in marketing, sales, creative, or communications for a real estate developer — hospitality, residential, mixed-use, luxury — you're closer to serious money than almost anyone in your field. But nobody sat you down and explained how it actually works. That's not your fault. It's just how this industry operates.

14 lessons. No MBA required. No equations to memorise. Just plain explanations of the concepts that shape every project you work on — written the way a colleague who actually likes you would explain it over a long coffee. Read one on your lunch break. Come back tomorrow for another.

By the end, you won't be a finance person. But you'll understand what the finance people are saying — and that changes everything about how you show up.

CJ
CJ Jondonero — real estate advisory and capital markets. I came up through the creative and marketing side of this industry. I built this because nobody handed it to me, and I think that was a mistake.
